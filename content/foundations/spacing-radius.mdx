We use the **shadcn/ui default token scales** for spacing, radius, and elevation — deliberately unmodified.

Every deviation we don't make is consistency we get for free, and a vocabulary AI agents already know. Values live in the shadcn documentation; controls TOK-1, TOK-2, and TOK-3 bind every product to them.

## Rhythm, not monotony

The scale gives you values; rhythm is still your job. Related items group tighter than unrelated ones — one spacing value everywhere is an AI-slop tell (SLP-7).

## Radius

Corner radii come from the shadcn radius scale (TOK-3). Cards top out around 12–16px; reserve full-pill for tags and buttons. Nested radii: child radius ≤ parent radius, concentric so curves align.
