## Production is controlled

All portfolio illustration is generated in **Midjourney** with the brand style reference on every prompt — no freehand styles, no other generators:

```
--sref 2544305963::2 4104856457
```

Working rules:

- The SREF goes on every prompt, weights unchanged.
- Describe the **subject** (a real teacher moment) and let the SREF carry the style — don't restate style keywords that fight it.
- An off-brand result is **regenerated, not retouched**.
- Approved results enter the shared illustration library; products pull from the library, not ad-hoc generations.

**Do:** warm palette anchored in brand colour · natural human proportions · soft edges, rounded forms · subtle hand-drawn texture.

**Don't:** corporate flat-pack style · cartoonish proportions · sharp aggressive angles · neon or harsh colours · hand-coded SVG mascots as fallbacks — ship no illustration rather than a sketchy one.
