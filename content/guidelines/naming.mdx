---
title: Naming
description: A name that requires explanation has already failed.
status: settled
---

Every feature, page, and label is a brand touchpoint. A teacher meeting a feature for the first time should know exactly what it does.

**Do:** plain language teachers already know · name by function, not metaphor · specific and descriptive · test names with real teachers.

**Don't:** portmanteau words · technical jargon or acronyms · cleverness over clarity · internal codenames in UI (CNT-2).

| Good | Bad | Why |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Class Planner | SyncFlow | Instantly clear vs. requires explanation |
| Student Notes | InsightHub | Obvious purpose vs. ambiguous and corporate |

Keep nouns consistent: introduce as few unique terms as possible, and never call the same thing two names in different modules (Invisible Seams).
